    FAQ

    What is PantheraNFT?

    Panthera is a community-led NFT project with a mission to upgrade the standards and ethics of charity organizations. Each week, Panthera auctions unique NFTs to raise funds for partner charities that have agreed to follow our guidelines to improve transparency and ethics.

    How does Panthera select its partner charities?

    We carefully select partner charities that align with our mission and ethical standards. We conduct due diligence to ensure they meet our guidelines and establish agreements for transparency and accountability. Our NFT holders also have a say in which charity organizations we work with.

    How does Panthera ensure transparency and accountability?

    We require our partner charities to provide regular reporting and updates on the impact of our donations. We also have a governance system that allows our NFT holders to participate in decision-making processes related to partner selection, funding allocation, and other key decisions.

    What makes Panthera different from other NFT projects?

    Panthera is unique in that we focus on using NFTs to raise funds for charity partners that meet our ethical standards. We also have a governance system that allows our NFT holders to participate in decision-making processes related to the project.

    How can I stay up-to-date with Panthera?

    You can follow us on social media or visit our website to stay up-to-date with our latest news and updates. You can also sign up for our newsletter to receive regular updates on our progress.
